"What is a Webologist?"
I hear you ask.

A Webologist is a term I invented for myself, as someone who studies the culture, people, technologies, and impacts of the World-Wide Web and the Internet. I am a practitioner of software development, including building web applications in Rails, Angular, PHP, and other languages and frameworks.

I am also a teacher of Software Engineering as a Craft (aka SWAAC) which I coach, consult, teach, and share with others to help them discover this fun, exciting, and rewarding field so I have more people to have fun with.
